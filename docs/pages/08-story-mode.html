8 Story Mode

Problem → Vision → Phases with KPIs, idea cards, and benefits

The story object adds a wizard-style narrative layer. When present, a "Story" button appears. Clicking it (or using ?story=true) enters a slide-based walkthrough.

Story Pipeline

1
Problem
Headline, description, impact metric, evidence links, risks
2
Vision
Summary, description, KPI targets with confidence intervals
3
Phase Slides
Per-phase: owner, timebox, status, description, idea cards
4
KPI HUD
Live running totals update as you advance through phases

Story Sub-Objects

problem
headline, description, impactMetric {kpiId, value, unit}, evidence [{label, url}], scope [nodeIds], risks []
vision
summary, description, kpiTargets [{kpiId, min, max, confidence, horizon}], acceptanceCriteria []
kpis
id, label, unit, direction (higher/lower_is_better), baseline, current, format ("0", "0.1f", "$0.00", "qualitative")
phases
phaseRef, label, order, owner, timebox, status (completed/active/planned), description
ideaCards
id, title, owner, hypothesis, linkedNodes [], linkedFlows [], phases [], status, confidence, expectedKpiImpacts [{kpiId, delta, confidence}]
benefits
id, title, phaseId, kpiId, targetRange {min, max}, boundNodes [] — click to highlight nodes for 3s

KPI Direction & Colors

Deltas are signed numbers. The KPI's direction field determines color: a positive delta on a "higher_is_better" KPI is green, while a positive delta on a "lower_is_better" KPI is red.

Qualitative KPIs

Use "format": "qualitative" for non-numeric metrics. Accumulated deltas map to labels:
