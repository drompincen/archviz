1 What is ArchViz?

Animated architecture diagrams — from JSON to interactive canvas

The Problem
Architecture diagrams are static images that go stale immediately. Sequence flows live in separate tools. There's no single view that shows what is built, how it connects, and why it matters — all in one place.
The Solution
Define your architecture in a single JSON file. ArchViz renders a spatial canvas with animated message flows, a sequence diagram, a story mode for stakeholder walkthroughs, and a KPI dashboard that ties architecture to business outcomes.

Who Benefits?

Architects & Tech Leads
Visualize infrastructure layers with zones, reveal architecture progressively with phases, and animate request flows to validate design decisions.
Engineering Managers
Walk stakeholders through a transformation story — problem, vision, phased roadmap. KPI HUD shows projected business impact at each phase.
Product & Delivery
Benefits panel ties architecture changes to outcomes. Export PDF for offline review. Phase slider shows what's built vs. what's planned.
One JSON, six views: spatial canvas, sequence diagram, story narrative, benefits panel, KPI dashboard, and PDF export — all from the same data source.
